What Is UNAI?


Universitas Advent Indonesia(UNAI) is a Higher Education institution managed by Sevent-day Adventist Church of western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. in the past, this school was founded for the education of evangelist of the Sevent-day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

1) Faculty of Philosophy
2) Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
3) Faculty of Economics
4) Faculty of Nursing
5) Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
6) Faculty of Information Technology
